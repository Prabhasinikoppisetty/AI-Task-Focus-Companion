# 🚀 AI Task & Focus Companion

> **Your AI-powered productivity workspace for smarter planning, deep focus, and stress-free deadlines.**

AI Task & Focus Companion is a modern, glassmorphic productivity application designed to eliminate cognitive overload, defeat procrastination, and help you achieve peak focus. It combines AI assistance, intelligent task prioritization, deadline prediction, habit tracking, and immersive focus tools into one seamless workspace.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🤖 AVA – AI Focus Companion
Your proactive AI productivity coach that actively helps you stay on track.

- Break down large projects into manageable tasks
- Intelligent task planning and scheduling
- Emergency rescheduling assistance
- Context-aware recommendations based on tasks and priorities

---

### 📊 Interactive Eisenhower Matrix

Organize work using the proven Urgent vs Important framework.

- Drag & drop task management
- Automatic quadrant classification
- Reduce decision fatigue
- Quickly identify high-impact work

---

### 🍅 Pomodoro Focus Shield

A distraction-free workspace designed for deep work.

- Fullscreen Focus Mode
- Built-in Pomodoro timer
- Play, pause, and reset controls
- Clean minimal interface
- Smooth focus transitions

---

### 🚨 Deadline Risk Analyzer

Prevent last-minute deadline stress.

- Compare estimated effort with available time
- Capacity-based workload analysis
- Visual deadline risk indicators
- Identify projects that require immediate attention

---

### ⚡ Emergency Rescue Sprint

Recover when you're behind schedule.

- Automatically prioritize important work
- Postpone low-priority tasks
- Generate a focused recovery schedule
- Stay productive under tight deadlines

---

### 📈 Habit Tracker & Flow Analytics

Build consistency through measurable progress.

- Daily habit tracking
- Productivity streaks
- Weekly focus insights
- Interactive charts and analytics
- Task completion statistics
